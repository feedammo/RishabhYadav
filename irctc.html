IRCTC Autofill Tool

IRCTC opens up train reservations at 8 AM, 10AM(AC-Tatkal) & 11AM(Tatkal-Sleeper). If you have ever tried booking tickets on IRCTC, you might have failed to get confirm tickets booked. Train Tickets got booked in matter of minutes. The IRCTC Autofill tool improves your chances of getting a confirmed train ticket, as it saves your valuable time spent entering the passenger details.

First open the reservation form, fill-in the passenger details and then click the Amaze Me button. Now drag the "Amaze Me" button to the bookmarks toolbar of your browser.

When you are on the Indian Railways website, click the Amaze Me button in your bookmarks to fill the reservation form with one click. It's as simple as that.
The IRCTC Autofill Tool is developed by Rishabh Yadav and is not affiliated to Indian Railways. The tool respects your privacy and your data is neither shared nor uploaded anywhere, it just stays on your computer. These pages are hosted on Github.io.
